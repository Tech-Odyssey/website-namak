For The Foodies
By The Foodies

During the First Lockdown, Many Food trends emerged as all our favourite restaurants were closed, and people had to go on the hunt of new recipes so that they could fulfill their cravings. We had to find a solution for them at home.

Namak Shamak, solves this problem by recommending multiple Recipes based on your choices, after which you can choose to share and discuss the same with others having similar choices.
